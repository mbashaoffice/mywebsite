Project Overview

Project Description

The Early Warning System (EWS) for banks is designed to proactively identify potential risks such as fraud, financial instability, or compliance issues. By setting triggers for specific thresholds or patterns in transaction data, the system can alert bank officials in real time, enabling swift intervention to mitigate risks. EWS helps monitor unusual financial activities, such as large transactions or irregular account behaviors, offering banks a mechanism to prevent fraud, avoid operational disruptions, and ensure compliance with regulations. This system is often integrated with banking core systems and can involve advanced algorithms for better accuracy
